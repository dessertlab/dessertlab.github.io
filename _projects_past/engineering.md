### Subjects
Engineering Ingegneria Informatica S.p.a and CINI consortium.

### Description

The project main activities are devoted to problems of content adaptation in the context of i) mobile devices and networks, ii) accessibility for disable people, and iii) RFID technologies sperimentation.
Engineering Informatica S.p.A sponsors the CINI consortium with 3 annual fellowship grants for this project. The study and research activity takes place at both the CINI-ITEM laboratory at Naples, and the Engineering laboratory at Torre Annunziata (Naples).
